I wish that I may never forget

This work traces an effort to hold an image in place as its surface changes. Each leaf bears a chlorotype print made from the same source image; one is documented shortly after printing, the other after a year of decay, and material breakdown. As the leaf degrades, parts of the image persist while others dissolve. Medium: chlorotype on leaf · Digital scans · (2024)
